Code of Conduct

The University of Amateur Radio club primary includes and supports all members of the University of Waterloo community. We are committed to providing a friendly, safe and welcoming environment for all, regardless of gender, sexual orientation, ability, ethnicity, socioeconomic status, and religion (or lack thereof).UWARC Code of Conduct

All members and visitors of the club (and its activities) are subject to the expectations detailed in our Code of Conduct.
We strive for an open and welcome environment for all members of the University of Waterloo community.
